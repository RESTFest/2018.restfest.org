Keynote

Your API’s Disappearing Act
Keynote: Friday, September 28th

APIs are often the gateways to get and post data, and delivering insights based on that data. Even if your API doesn’t deliver insights directly, chances are someone somewhere is using your API or related APIs to make sense of the world. But what does that world look like? Is it inclusive? Is it true? This talk will look at ways in which we not only marginalize/ostracize users but then build ongoing, ever-evolving insights based on completely inaccurate data. We’ll also examine ways in which your API or third-party APIs you consume may be contributing to this.

Lorinda is a Director of Developer Marketing at Capital One Platform Services.
